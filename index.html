Rules:

  • Take every interview and "be modest."
  • Do not upgrade your office size until you have 5x the amount required.
  • Always complete upgrades suggested by employees.
  • Every 2 projects, leads/heads get a vacation.
  • Whenever a worker's meter is under 30%, he gets a vacation.
  • Every game must have a marketing campaign once marketing is researched.
  • Every game must have a researched report.
  • New topics must be unlocked consistently.
